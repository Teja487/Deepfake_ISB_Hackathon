Weather refers to the atmospheric conditions in a specific area at a particular time. It includes various factors like temperature, humidity, precipitation, wind speed, and cloud cover. These conditions can change rapidly and vary greatly depending on geographical location and season. Weather affects daily activities, agriculture, transportation, and even moods. It can range from sunny, warm days to severe storms or extreme cold, with events like hurricanes, snowstorms, and droughts having significant impacts on human life and the environment. Monitoring weather patterns helps in predicting short-term forecasts and preparing for adverse conditions.
